## 🎯 Purpose

A **standalone marketing landing page** to promote the Being Home Foods recipe app — drive downloads/signups, build trust, and showcase features.

---

## 📱 What the App Currently Has (Features to Market)

### Core Features
| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Recipe Discovery** | Browse, search, and filter recipes by cuisine, category, and dietary type (Veg, Non-Veg, Vegan, Egg) |
| **Popular Recipes** | Curated popular recipes section on the homepage |
| **"What to Cook?" (Random Recipe)** | AI-powered random recipe suggestion — tap a button, get a surprise recipe |
| **Recipe Details** | Full recipe view with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, nutrition info, YouTube tutorials, serving adjuster |
| **User Submissions** | Anyone can submit their own recipes with images |
| **Ratings & Reviews** | Star rating system for community feedback |
| **Favorites** | Save and organize your favorite recipes |
| **Browse by Cuisine** | Indian 🍛, Italian 🍝, Asian 🥢, Mexican 🌮, Healthy 🥗, Bakery 🧁 |
| **My Kitchen (Knowledge Bank)** | Educational section about food habits, dietary types, and food groups |
| **Ingredient-Based Search** | Find recipes based on ingredients you have |
| **Notifications** | Stay updated on new recipes and activity |
| **User Profiles** | Personal profiles with food interest preferences |
| **YouTube Shorts Carousel** | Embedded cooking video shorts for quick inspiration |

### Design & UX Highlights
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Modern cooking-themed login with hero imagery
- Floating bottom navigation with centered "What to Cook" button
- Dark/light mode support
- Image viewer for recipe photos
- Serving size scaler for ingredients

## 🏗️ Marketing Site — Recommended Sections

### 1. **Hero Section**
- Bold headline: e.g., *"Your Kitchen, Your Rules"* or *"Discover Recipes You'll Love"*
- Subheadline emphasizing community + ingredient-based discovery
- CTA buttons: "Get Started" / "Explore Recipes"
- Hero image: app screenshot mockup or cooking lifestyle photo
- Subtle animation (fade-in or parallax)

### 2. **Feature Highlights** (3–6 cards)
- 🔍 **Smart Search** — Find recipes by ingredient, cuisine, or diet
- 🎲 **What to Cook?** — Can't decide? Get a random recipe suggestion
- 📝 **Share Your Recipes** — Submit and showcase your own creations
- ⭐ **Rate & Review** — Community-driven ratings
- ❤️ **Save Favorites** — Build your personal recipe collection
- 🎓 **My Kitchen** — Learn about food habits and nutrition

### 3. **App Screenshots / Demo**
- Carousel or grid of 3–5 app screenshots showing:
  - Home page with popular recipes
  - Recipe detail page
  - "What to Cook" random recipe modal
  - Browse by cuisine grid
  - Create recipe form

### 4. **How It Works** (3 steps)
1. **Browse or Search** — Find recipes by ingredient, cuisine, or mood
2. **Cook Along** — Follow step-by-step instructions with video guides
3. **Share & Rate** — Submit your recipes, rate others, build community

### 5. **Cuisine Showcase**
- Visual grid of supported cuisines with emojis/images
- Indian, Italian, Asian, Mexican, Healthy, Bakery
- Each links to a filtered recipe view

### 6. **Testimonials / Social Proof**
- User quotes (can be placeholder initially)
- Stats: "X+ recipes", "Y+ home cooks", "Z+ cuisines"

### 7. **Community / UGC Section**
- Highlight that anyone can contribute recipes
- Show a few featured user-submitted recipes
- CTA: "Share Your First Recipe"

### 8. **YouTube / Video Section**
- Embed a cooking video or show the shorts carousel concept
- "Quick cooking inspiration in 60 seconds"

### 9. **CTA / Sign-Up Banner**
- Sticky or mid-page CTA: "Join thousands of home cooks"
- Email/phone signup or direct link to app registration

### 10. **Footer**
- Links: About Us, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions
- Contact info (from Info page): email + phone
- Social media icons (placeholder)
- Brand logo

## 🎨 Design Direction for Marketing Site

### Tone
- **Warm, inviting, food-forward** — think Licious, Swiggy, or Zomato landing pages
- Modern with generous whitespace and food photography
- Trust-building with community emphasis

### Typography
- Display font: Bold, warm serif or modern geometric sans (e.g., Playfair Display + DM Sans)
- Avoid generic Inter/Poppins

### Color Palette
- Use the app's existing green-based palette (`--primary: forest green`)
- Warm accents: orange/amber for CTAs
- Cream/off-white backgrounds for a homey feel

### Imagery
- High-quality cooking/food lifestyle photos
- App mockups on phone frames
- Emoji accents for cuisine categories

### Animations
- Scroll-triggered fade-ins (framer-motion)
- Parallax hero image
- Counter animations for stats
